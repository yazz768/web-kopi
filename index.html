Discover Our Story

Founded in the heart of the city, La Day Le has been crafting exceptional coffee experiences since 2010. Our commitment to quality begins with selecting the finest coffee beans from sustainable sources around the world.

Each cup we serve is a testament to our dedication to the art of coffee making, combining traditional methods with modern innovation to create the perfect brew.

Our Passion & Expertise

At La Day Le, we believe that great coffee is an art form. Our master baristas are trained in the finest techniques, ensuring each cup meets our exacting standards of excellence.

We've created a warm and inviting space where coffee lovers can gather, work, or simply enjoy a moment of tranquility with their favorite brew. Our dedication to customer service is matched only by our passion for coffee.
